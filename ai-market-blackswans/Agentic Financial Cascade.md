## Event 16: n8n Valuation Explosion (2025) — The Agentic Financial Cascade

### 1. Vectors Hit
* **Tech Discontinuity:** The hard pivot from passive generative AI (chatbots) to agentic AI/orchestration (systems that execute complex, multi-step actions across different software). 
* **Financial:** A textbook pre/post-shock valuation cascade. Driven by the agent wave, n8n's revenue grew 4x in 8 months, causing its valuation to skyrocket 7x—from $350 million in its March 2025 Series B to $2.5 billion by its October 2025 Series C (with further massive backing from SAP pushing it over $5.2B).

### 2. Key Actors Affected
* **Felt it first:** Legacy SaaS integration platforms (like Zapier). They suddenly found their rigid, linear automation models entirely outclassed by open-source, AI-native agent orchestrators capable of dynamic reasoning.
* **Felt it hardest:** Venture capital allocators. They were caught entirely off guard by the speed at which enterprise value migrated from the LLM model layer (which was rapidly commoditizing) up to the workflow orchestration layer.
* **Insulated / Beneficiaries:** Enterprise operations teams and NVIDIA. NVentures directly participated in n8n's massive October round, beautifully closing the loop—NVIDIA profited from the underlying compute scarcity *and* the downstream software orchestration explosion.

### 3. Precursor Signals
* **OpenAI's "Function Calling" Release (June 2023):** Proved that LLMs could reliably output structured JSON to trigger external APIs, the foundational technical requirement for autonomous agents.
* **The Limitations of Chat:** By late 2024, enterprise churn rates for simple "wrapper" AI startups spiked, heavily signaling that businesses would only pay for AI that *did the work automatically*, not just AI that drafted text.

### 4. Shock Duration
* **Months (Acute Upside Shock).** The financial repricing was instantaneous. In a span of roughly 120 days, the private markets completely re-evaluated the enterprise automation sector, fundamentally resetting revenue multiples for any company successfully deploying agentic workflows.

### 5. Recurrence & Swan Classification
* **White Swan.** The historical pattern of enterprise tech dictates that whenever a new compute primitive emerges (cloud, mobile, AI), the middleware/orchestration layer that connects it to legacy databases captures massive value. The only surprise was the sheer compression of the timeline (7x valuation jump in 7 months).
